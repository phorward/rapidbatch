[Echo_Title], [Confirm_Title]
 
Description: Defines the title bar texts that are displayed at ECHO (variable: [Echo_Title]) and CONFIRM (variable: [Confirm_Title]).

By default, these values are "RapidBATCH Echo" and "RapidBATCH Confirm".
