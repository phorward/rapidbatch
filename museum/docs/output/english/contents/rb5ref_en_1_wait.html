WAIT
 
Syntax: WAIT milliseconds
Description: Suspends the script execution for the given time of milliseconds until it continues.
One second is 1000 milliseconds.
Examples: shell 'calc.exe', 'show'
rem wait for two seconds...
wait '2000'
echo 'You can calculate now ;)...'
end
