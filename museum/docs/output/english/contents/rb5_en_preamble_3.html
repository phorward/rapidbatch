Who creates RapidBATCH?
 
RapidBATCH is a growing programming language that is continuously been developed further.

Inventor and developer of RapidBATCH is the tiny software company J.M.K S.F. Software Technologies from Unna in Germany (near Dortmund), whose founder is the RapidBATCH core developer and rookie entrepreneur Jan Max Meyer.

Our goal is the development and deployment of powerful, revolutionary programming systems and platforms with a high quality and excellent performance, to bring everyone the flavor and fun of programming closer. We think we met our goal so far with this new, available version 5 of the RapidBATCH scripting platform now.

We hope that RapidBATCH fits your demands and requirements, enabling your to apply it at the best possible rate.

But for all that, your opinion is important to us: If you have any suggestions or comments for coming RapidBATCH versions and releases, just send us a mail with your ideas.

For further information to J.M.K S.F. Software Technologies, our products and individual software offerings, visit our web-site at http://www.jmksf.com!
