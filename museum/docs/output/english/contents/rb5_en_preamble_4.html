How to buy the Professional Edition?
 

What is the advantage of RapidBATCH Professional Edition?
The RapidBATCH Professional Edition is the more powerful version of the RapidBATCH scripting platform for advanced and commercial users. RapidBATCH Professional Edition enabled you to compile your RapidBATCH scripts into stand-alone EXE-files that can be deployed to others and are runable on any Windows machine without an installed RapidBATCH scripting platform.
Additionally, RapidBATCH Professional Edition comes with an integrated script-debugger that enables you to run scripts stepwise and lets you view and change the content of variables direclty at run-time.
The included software-tool "Visual Dialog Designer" supports you on designing and creating your individual dialogs and application windows for your scripts quickly and easily.

Customers of the RapidBATCH Professional Edition receive free updates on any future Professional Edition of the RapidBATCH 5.x scripting platform and get exclusive personal e-mail support if you're having troubles.

What is the pricing?
RapidBATCH Professional Edition is a very cheap development solution for your business. For only a price of $24.95 per license, you're getting the complete RapidBATCH Professional Edition including all features.

Where to buy?
RapidBATCH Professional Edition can be ordered and directly downloaded via credit-card from the official RapidBATCH Homepage at http://www.rapidbatch.com/.
